Smart Loading of Large Data Sets: Smartphone Time Series

A time series for 2 weeks' worth of a single time series - CPU usage measured at 5 second intervals - amounts to about 6 MB of data. Downsampled at a 10:1 ratio, this is more reasonable in size, but still slow to load. This page illustrates how to draw a chart and give the user something to look at while the data is loading. (The superior approach is to tuck data into bundles of adjustible size and amount, accessible by JSON using a REST API.)
